## Legal & Compliance

### Amazon Associates TOS Compliance

**Allowed:**
- Linking to Amazon with affiliate tag
- Showing Amazon reviews on your site
- Comparing prices (Amazon vs direct)

**Not Allowed:**
- Masking affiliate links
- Incentivizing purchases through Amazon link
- Claiming Amazon endorsement

**Best Practice:**
- Disclose affiliate relationship: "We earn a commission if you purchase through Amazon (at no extra cost to you)"

### Email Marketing Compliance (CAN-SPAM)

**Required:**
- Clear "From" name (your brand)
- Accurate subject lines (no deception)
- Unsubscribe link in every email
- Physical mailing address in footer
- Honor unsubscribes within 10 days

**Best Practice:**
- Double opt-in for quality list
- Segment by interests
- Don't buy email lists
- Respect preferences

### Facebook Ads Policy

**Allowed:**
- Educational content about tax strategies
- Testimonials (if authentic)
- Before/after results (if documented)

**Not Allowed:**
- False claims ("eliminate all tax debt")
- Misleading guarantees
- Targeting based on sensitive categories

**Best Practice:**
- Use "Learn more" or "Get guide" CTAs
- Avoid sensational language
- Include disclaimer if needed
